Capital Letter

Ⲇ

Small Letter

ⲇ

Write like a small 'd'

🗣️ How to Say It

In English

"th" (like in 'this')

In Arabic

ذ (thal)

Sound Symbol

[ð]

📖 Example Word

🌟 Fun Facts

📜 Origin

This letter comes from the Greek alphabet!

🔤 Fourth Letter

Delta is the fourth letter of the Coptic alphabet!

🎯 Soft Sound

It makes a soft 'th' sound, like in 'this' or 'that'!
