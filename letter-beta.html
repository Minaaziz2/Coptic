🗣️ How to Say It

In English

"v" (like in 'very') or "b" at word end

In Arabic

ب (ba)

Sound Symbol

[β] / [v]

🌟 Fun Facts

📜 Origin

This letter comes from the Greek alphabet, like the English 'B'!

🔤 Second Letter

Beta is the second letter of the Coptic alphabet!

🎯 Sound Change

It sounds like 'v' in the middle of words, but 'b' at the end!
