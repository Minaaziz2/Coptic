Capital Letter

Small Letter

ⲁ

Write like a small 'a'

🗣️ How to Say It

In English

"ah" (like in 'father')

In Arabic

أ (alif)

Sound Symbol

[a]

📖 Example Word

ⲁⲅⲁⲡⲏ
(agapy)
💖 Love 💖 محبة (mahaba)

🌟 Fun Facts

📜 Origin

This letter comes from the Greek alphabet, just like the English 'A'!

🔤 First Letter

Alpha is the first letter of the Coptic alphabet, just like 'A' in English!

🎯 Easy to Remember

It looks and sounds just like the letter 'A' you already know!
